Web portal for the company **Panecons**. Based on BoltCMS (Symfony PHP), it includes a custom admin panel for content management and the implementation of extensions for contact forms and Captcha protection. Data was migrated from the previous CMS (WordPress), a vulnerability exploited by certain spam bots was fixed, and a new responsive design was implemented. User manuals for site management and administration were delivered.
